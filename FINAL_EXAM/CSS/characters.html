Main Characters

Protagonists

Asta

The underdog hero with no magic, wielding Anti-Magic swords. His dream is to become Wizard King

Yuno

Asta’s rival and childhood friend, blessed with immense wind magic and royal lineage

Yami Sukehiro

Captain of the Black Bulls, known for his dark magic and laid-back but deadly fighting style

Julius Nova Chrono

The current Wizard King, master of time magic, central to the film’s theme of leadership

Secre

Asta’s companion, formerly a servant of the first Wizard King, with sealing magic

Nozel Silva

Captain of the Silver Eagles, a proud noble with mercury magic

Antagonists

Conrad Leto

Former Wizard King who turned against the Clover Kingdom.

Edward Avalaché

Known for his ice-based magic and formidable combat skills.

Princia Funnybunny

Uses unique magic abilities that make her a dangerous foe.

Jester Garandros

A revived Wizard King with eccentric personality and deadly magic.

Lucius Zogratis

Member of the Zogratis siblings and a central villain in the Black Clover universe.

Lucifero

the King of Devils, ultimate threat.
